What is Gold, Silver, Bronze?

Gold, Silver, Bronze is an initiative to improve the quality of courses on Citybit. This will improve the experience of the learner and lead to better success rates and student retention. Each course is graded with a medal: Gold, Silver, Bronze or In Development.

What do these grades mean?

 A Bronze course is regularly accessed by at least half the enrolled students, has a full compliment of documents to support the learner, including all course hand-outs.

A Silver course includes all of the bronze course elements but in addition contains a full selection of interactive material to engage the learner.

A Gold course contains all elements of a bronze and silver course but in addition contains activities which encourage the learner to collaborate with their fellow course mates and the teacher. In this way the learner contributes to the content of the course.

A course deemed to be In Development falls short of a bronze course.

How can I improve my course grade?

The easiest way to improve your course grade and better support your students, is to contact a member of the e-Learning team. We can advise you on good practise in e-Learning and proven techinques to engage your students.
