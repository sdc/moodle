Restoring Groups

This setting enables you to choose whether you want to restore groups and groupings (if enabled).

The possible options are:

  • No - Do not restore groups
  • Yes - Restore groups
  • None - Do not restore groups or groupings
  • Groupings only - Groupings are restored, empty, with the groupings selected for grouping activities maintained
  • Groups and groupings - Groups and groupings are restored, with all related settings maintained

Group mode for course and activities

Whichever option is chosen, the group mode for the course and for activities is maintained.
